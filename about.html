shtfy is a service run by aykit.

We at aykit hate data retention and love open-source. That is why shtfy is not storing unnessecary data. shtfy is storing only:

ID of Databaseentry
Every DB-entry has an unique id (this is common with databases)
All URLs shortified
Every URL that has been shortified is saved to prevent multiple shortified versions and save us good-looking URLs.
Hits
We save how often a shortified URL has been visited.
Piwik visit tracking - Anonymised visits
Knowing how many people visit our service is always good for morale. However, we do our best to track as little as possible: We save your IP with the last digits anonymised (192.168.0.xxx) and we respect your Browser's Do-Not-Track setting. If you still don't trust us, we recommend usage of Ghostery or similar software.

Oh yeah, and we are advertisement-free.
